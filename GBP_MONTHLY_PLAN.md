## Review Collection System (Run Every Month)

### The Process
1. Finish a job
2. Same day, text the customer:

> "Hi [name], really glad you're happy with the [project]. If you have 2 minutes, a Google review would mean the world to us. Here's the link: [your direct review link]. Thanks so much!"

3. If no response after 3 days, send one gentle follow-up
4. **Respond to EVERY review** within 24 hours (Google tracks this)

### Review Response Templates

**5-star review:**
> "Thanks so much [name], it was a pleasure working on your [project type]. Really glad you're happy with the result. Don't hesitate to get in touch if you need anything in the future!"

**4-star review:**
> "Thanks for the review [name]. Glad you're happy overall. If there's anything we can improve on, we'd love to hear -- always looking to get better."

**Negative review (if ever):**
> "Sorry to hear this [name]. We take every bit of feedback seriously. Could you give us a call on 07948 643391 so we can make things right?"
